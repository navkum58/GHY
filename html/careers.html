GHY University

The GHY University program was established in 2009 as a way to continue the intellectual growth of our associates and better their personal life.

Our goal is to enhance our associate’s technical knowledge of the trade and customs industry, customer service and the professional and business invironment. As of 2013, we have officially begun offering some GHY University courses to our clients.
